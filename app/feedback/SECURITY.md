## üìú Coordinated Disclosure Policy

We follow industry-standard **coordinated disclosure** practices:

1. **Report Received**: You submit the vulnerability report
2. **Acknowledgment**: We confirm receipt within 48 hours
3. **Assessment**: Our security team investigates and validates
4. **Remediation**: We develop and test a fix
5. **Patch Release**: Security update deployed to production
6. **Public Disclosure**: Coordinated announcement (with your credit, if desired)

### üîí Disclosure Timeline

- **Critical/High**: 30-60 days from initial report
- **Medium/Low**: 60-90 days from initial report
- **Early Disclosure**: Possible if actively exploited in the wild
